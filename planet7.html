Interesting Properties/Factoids

2MASS J1119–1137 is actually a binary system of two objects, each with a mass of around 4 times Jupiter's. This makes 2MASS J1119–1137 the lowest-mass binary system discovered so far in the universe!

How It Was Discovered

2MASS J1119–1137 was discovered using NASA's Wide-Field Infrared Survey Explorer, along with help from other telescopes. It was found thanks to its unusually high levels of infrared radiation.
