Mass

Distance Away/Where They Are

7.2 light-years away

Physical Descriptors

WISE 0855−0714 has a temperature of -48 to 13 degrees Celcius

Interesting Properties/Factoids

WISE 0855−0714 is the closest rogue planet to Earth! It is also as cold as the North Pole! Finally, scientists believe it has potential to be a brown dwarf, due to the commonality of brown dwarfs, although they are confident it is a rogue planet due to it's smaller mass.

How It Was Discovered

WISE 0855−0714 was discovered using two infrared telescopes, NASA's Wide-Field Infrared Explorer (WISE) and the Spitzer telescope. These telescopes, by observing the universe in infrared light, allow NASA to see parts of the universe unseen in visible light.
