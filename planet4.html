Interesting Properties/Factoids

The object has no solar companion for 3.9 AU! Also, given it's large mass, scientists, while confident it is a rogue planet, believe it could be a brown dwarf.

How It Was Discovered

OGLE-2017-BLG-0560 was discovered using a telescope that was built for the Optical Gravitational Lensing Experiment (OGLE) and can detect objects with masses on a planetary to stellar range.
